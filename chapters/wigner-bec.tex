\chapter{Wigner representation of BEC quantum dynamics}
\label{cha:wigner-bec}
% =============================================================================

In this chapter we will apply the field operator calculus and the functional Wigner transformation from \charef{wigner} along with the transformation theorems from \charef{wigner-spec} to the master equation of \abbrev{bec} dynamics.
We will discuss the applicability of the truncation approximation, which is required to get rid of the possible negativity of the Wigner functional.
Finally, we will employ the correspondences from \appref{fpe-sde} to derive stochastic equations which can be solved numerically.
